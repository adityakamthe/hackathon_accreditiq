

NBA Self-Assessment Report (SAR)
## Framework & Checklist
Format Version: 4.0 Reference: AISSMS College of Engineering Sample Report Structure
## Part A: Institutional Information
Focus: Administrative, Legal, and Organizational Foundation
● [ ] AICTE Approval Letters: Complete set of copies for all years since the institution's
inception.
● [ ] University Affiliation Letters: Most recent affiliation letter and historical records from
the parent university.
● [ ] Trust/Society Registration: Legal registration documents of the governing
body/society.
● [ ] Program Inventory: List of all programs (UG/PG), sanctioned intake, and accreditation
history (previous cycles).
● [ ] Staff and Student Statistics: Certified lists for the Current Academic Year (CAY),
CAYm1, and CAYm2.
● [ ] Institutional Map/Infrastructure: Approved building plans and land documents.
## Part B: Program Level Criteria
Criterion 1: Outcome-Based Curriculum (120 Marks)
## ● [ ] Vision & Mission Documentation:
○ [ ] Formal statements for both the Institute and the Department.
○ [ ] Meeting Minutes: Records of brainstorming sessions with stakeholders (DAB,
IQAC) used to define these statements.
○ [ ] Dissemination Proofs: Photos of posters in labs/halls, website screenshots,
brochures, and lab manuals.
● [ ] PEO Documentation: 3 to 5 defined Program Educational Objectives.
● [ ] Curriculum Structure: Syllabus copies (e.g., SPPU) and credit distribution tables.
● [ ] Gap Analysis: Reports identifying curricular gaps and proofs of "Content Beyond
Syllabus" (guest lectures, industrial visits).
Criterion 2: Outcome-Based Teaching-Learning (120 Marks)
● [ ] Academic Calendars: Both Institute-level and Department-specific.
● [ ] Course Files: Comprehensive files containing teaching plans, attendance records, and
lecture notes.
● [ ] Capstone Project Records: List of projects, guide allotment records, monitoring
sheets, and evaluation rubrics.
● [ ] Industry Interaction: Signed MOUs, industrial training certificates, and internship
completion letters for students.

Criterion 3: Outcome-Based Assessment (120 Marks)
● [ ] Assessment Tools: Samples of internal test papers, assignments, and tutorial sheets
mapped to Course Outcomes (COs).
● [ ] Attainment Data: Spreadsheets/Calculations showing CO, PO, and PSO attainment.
● [ ] Indirect Surveys: Records of exit surveys (graduating students), employer feedback,
and alumni surveys.
## Criterion 4: Students' Performance (120 Marks)
● [ ] Admission Records: Entrance rank data (JEE/CET) and merit lists for the last three
years.
● [ ] Result Analysis: University result ledgers and API (Academic Performance Index)
calculation sheets.
● [ ] Placement & Higher Ed: Appointment letters, GATE/GRE/CAT scorecards, and
admission proofs for higher studies.
● [ ] Professional Societies: Membership lists and award certificates from student
chapters (IEEE, SAE, CSI, etc.).
## Criterion 5: Faculty Information (100 Marks)
● [ ] Faculty Profiles: Detailed resumes including PAN, Aadhar, joining dates, and
designations.
● [ ] Qualification Proofs: Ph.D. and PG degree certificates.
● [ ] Retention Data: Salary statements, Form 16, or appointment orders proving faculty
tenure and retention.
## Criterion 6: Faculty Contributions (120 Marks)
● [ ] Development Records: Certificates for FDPs, STTPs, and workshops
attended/organized.
● [ ] Research Output: Copies of journals/conference papers (Scopus/WoS indexed) and
book chapters.
● [ ] Funding & Intellectual Property: Grant letters for sponsored projects and patent
certificates.
Criterion 7: Facilities and Technical Support (100 Marks)
● [ ] Lab Inventories: Stock registers, major equipment lists, and purchase invoices with
cost details.
● [ ] Safety & Maintenance: Fire safety certificates, first aid logs, maintenance registers,
and "Do's & Don'ts" signage.
## Criterion 8: Continuous Improvement (80 Marks)
● [ ] Action Taken Reports (ATR): Formal reports demonstrating how assessment data led
to program improvements.

● [ ] Academic Audits: Findings and compliance reports from internal and external audits.
## Part C: Institute Level Criteria
Criterion 9: Student Support and Governance (120 Marks)
● [ ] Governance: Governing Body and College Development Committee (CDC) meeting
minutes.
● [ ] Financials: Audited balance sheets and specific program budget utilization reports.
● [ ] Support Systems: Mentoring diaries/logs, counseling records, and anti-ragging
committee files.
● [ ] E-Governance: Proof of LMS (Google Classroom, Moodle) or ERP system usage.
## Part D: Declaration
● [ ] Signed Declaration: Official declaration by the Head of the Institution
(Principal/Director) verifying the accuracy of the SAR.
